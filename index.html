About Our School

Kiwumulo Primary School is a modern day and boarding primary school located in Kajansi. Our mission is to provide quality education, discipline, leadership, and innovation to all learners. We focus on academic excellence, moral values, and nurturing talent among pupils.

What We Offer

Day School

Affordable and quality education for day scholars with excellent teachers.

Boarding Section

Safe and comfortable boarding facilities for boys and girls.

Co-Curricular Activities

Sports, music, drama, debate, and computer lessons for holistic growth.

Admissions Open

Admissions are currently open for Nursery and Primary sections. We welcome pupils from all backgrounds to join our school community.

  • Modern learning environment

Founder Profile

Mr. Victor Kiwumulo is the founder and visionary behind Kiwumulo Primary School. He is passionate about education, leadership, digital innovation, and empowering young people through learning. His vision is to create a school that not only focuses on academic success but also develops discipline, creativity, and future-ready skills among learners.

Through his dedication to education and community development, he aims to transform lives by providing access to quality education in Kajansi and beyond.
